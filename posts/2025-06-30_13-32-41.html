Quote of the Day

"The most certain sign of wisdom is cheerfulness." — Michel de Montaigne

— Michel de Montaigne

Vocabulary Focus: certain

Definition: definite but not specified or identified.

Quiz: What does certain mean?

  1. An invisible person or thing; specifically, God, the Supreme Being.; A Rosicrucian; so called because avoiding declaration of his craft.
  2. Steadfast adherence to a strict moral or ethical code.; The state of being wholesome; unimpaired
  3. do away with, cause the destruction or undoing of.
  4. definite but not specified or identified.
Answer: D) definite but not specified or identified.

Vocabulary Focus: cheerfulness

Definition: The state of being cheerful; joy.

Quiz: What does cheerfulness mean?

  1. To sketch out, draw or trace an outline.; To depict, represent with pictures.
  2. (ditransitive) To obtain; to acquire.; To receive.
  3. The state of being cheerful; joy.
  4. connect closely and often incriminatingly.
Answer: C) The state of being cheerful; joy.
