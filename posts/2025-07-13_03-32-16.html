Vocabulary Focus: ignorant

Definition: uneducated in general; lacking knowledge or sophistication.

Quiz: What does ignorant mean?

  1. To give permission, power, or the legal right to do something.; To give someone more confidence and/or strength to do something, often by enabling them to increase their control over their own life or situation.
  2. One who dreams.; Someone whose beliefs are far from realistic.
  3. An advancement in rank or position.; Dissemination of information in order to increase its popularity.
  4. uneducated in general; lacking knowledge or sophistication.
Answer: D) uneducated in general; lacking knowledge or sophistication.
