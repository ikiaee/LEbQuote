Vocabulary Focus: Creativity

Definition: The ability to use imagination to produce a novel idea or product that is useful to society.

Quiz: What does Creativity mean?

  1. The ability to use imagination to produce a novel idea or product that is useful to society.
  2. At the end or conclusion; ultimately.; (sequence) To finish (with); lastly (in the present).
  3. The act of receiving, or the fact of having been received.; The fact of having received a blow, injury etc.
  4. (manner) In a complete manner; (degree) To the fullest extent or degree; totally.
Answer: A) The ability to use imagination to produce a novel idea or product that is useful to society.
