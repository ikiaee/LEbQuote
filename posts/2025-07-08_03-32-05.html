Vocabulary Focus: doubts

Definition: Disbelief or uncertainty (about something); a particular instance of such disbelief or uncertainty.; A point of uncertainty; a query.

Quiz: What does doubts mean?

  1. To cause to feel apprehension; great sadness, or fear; to deprive of energy; To render lifeless; to subdue; to disquiet.
  2. Disbelief or uncertainty (about something); a particular instance of such disbelief or uncertainty.; A point of uncertainty; a query.
  3. An invisible person or thing; specifically, God, the Supreme Being.; A Rosicrucian; so called because avoiding declaration of his craft.
  4. an expected outcome that is intended or that guides your planned actions.
Answer: B) Disbelief or uncertainty (about something); a particular instance of such disbelief or uncertainty.; A point of uncertainty; a query.
