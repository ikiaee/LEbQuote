Vocabulary Focus: simplest

Definition: Uncomplicated; taken by itself, with nothing added.; Without ornamentation; plain.

Quiz: What does simplest mean?

  1. Used as plural of person; a body of human beings considered generally or collectively; a group of two or more persons.; Persons forming or belonging to a particular group, such as a nation, class, ethnic group, country, family, etc.
  2. Uncomplicated; taken by itself, with nothing added.; Without ornamentation; plain.
  3. To make complex; to modify so as to make something intricate or difficult.
  4. A controlled behaviour; self-control.; A specific branch of knowledge or learning.
Answer: B) Uncomplicated; taken by itself, with nothing added.; Without ornamentation; plain.
