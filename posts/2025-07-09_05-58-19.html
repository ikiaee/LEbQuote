Vocabulary Focus: getting

Definition: (ditransitive) To obtain; to acquire.; To receive.

Quiz: What does getting mean?

  1. Something which lasts for a short period of time.; Lasting for a short period of time.
  2. greater in size or importance or degree.
  3. (ditransitive) To obtain; to acquire.; To receive.
  4. The ancient search for a universal panacea, and of the philosopher's stone, that eventually developed into chemistry.; The causing of any sort of mysterious sudden transmutation.
Answer: C) (ditransitive) To obtain; to acquire.; To receive.
