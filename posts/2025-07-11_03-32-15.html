Vocabulary Focus: unconscious

Definition: Unconscious mind; Not awake; having no awareness.

Quiz: What does unconscious mean?

  1. things of importance or concern.
  2. Not seen or discovered; invisible.; Unskilled; inexperienced.
  3. Unconscious mind; Not awake; having no awareness.
  4. A steam-powered heavy road roller; Any heavy road roller
Answer: C) Unconscious mind; Not awake; having no awareness.
