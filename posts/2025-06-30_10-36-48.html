Vocabulary Focus: simplicity

Definition: The state or quality of being simple

Quiz: What does simplicity mean?

  1. a visual representation of something.
  2. A feeling of extreme happiness or cheerfulness, especially related to the acquisition or expectation of something good.; Anything that causes such a feeling.
  3. occurring from time to time.
  4. The state or quality of being simple
Answer: D) The state or quality of being simple

Vocabulary Focus: patience

Definition: the capacity to accept or tolerate delay, problems, or suffering without becoming annoyed or anxious.

Quiz: What does patience mean?

  1. the act of traveling from one place to another.
  2. A soldier fit for duty; Having the power to produce a required effect or effects.
  3. the capacity to accept or tolerate delay, problems, or suffering without becoming annoyed or anxious.
  4. To resolve (a contest, problem, dispute, etc.); to choose, determine, or settle; To make a judgment, especially after deliberation
Answer: C) the capacity to accept or tolerate delay, problems, or suffering without becoming annoyed or anxious.
