Vocabulary Focus: frustrations

Definition: The feeling of annoyance when one's actions are criticized or hindered; The act of frustrating, or the state, or an instance of being frustrated

Quiz: What does frustrations mean?

  1. Uncomplicated; taken by itself, with nothing added.; Without ornamentation; plain.
  2. The feeling of annoyance when one's actions are criticized or hindered; The act of frustrating, or the state, or an instance of being frustrated
  3. That which comes from one's imagination.; The literary genre generally dealing with themes of magic and the supernatural, imaginary worlds and creatures, etc.
  4. (heading) Of time.; (heading) Unlimited or generalized extent, physical or otherwise.
Answer: B) The feeling of annoyance when one's actions are criticized or hindered; The act of frustrating, or the state, or an instance of being frustrated

Poem of the Day: My Shadow

by Robert Louis Stevenson

I have a little shadow that goes in and out with me,

And what can be the use of him is more than I can see.

He is very, very like me from the heels up to the head;

And I see him jump before me, when I jump into my bed.
