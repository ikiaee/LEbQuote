Quote of the Day

"Everything you are against weakens you. Everything you are for empowers you." — Wayne Dyer

— Wayne Dyer

Vocabulary Focus: weakens

Definition: To make weaker or less strong.; To become weaker or less strong.

Quiz: What does weakens mean?

  1. To make weaker or less strong.; To become weaker or less strong.
  2. An advancement in rank or position.; Dissemination of information in order to increase its popularity.
  3. The quality of being original or novel.; The capacity to think independently or be inventive.
  4. Not seen or discovered; invisible.; Unskilled; inexperienced.
Answer: A) To make weaker or less strong.; To become weaker or less strong.

Vocabulary Focus: empowers

Definition: To give permission, power, or the legal right to do something.; To give someone more confidence and/or strength to do something, often by enabling them to increase their control over their own life or situation.

Quiz: What does empowers mean?

  1. affect with wonder.
  2. remove from customary environment or associations.
  3. a state of equilibrium.
  4. To give permission, power, or the legal right to do something.; To give someone more confidence and/or strength to do something, often by enabling them to increase their control over their own life or situation.
Answer: D) To give permission, power, or the legal right to do something.; To give someone more confidence and/or strength to do something, often by enabling them to increase their control over their own life or situation.
