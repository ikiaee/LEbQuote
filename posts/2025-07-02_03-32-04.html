Vocabulary Focus: relationship

Definition: Connection or association; the condition of being related.; The links between the x-values and y-values of ordered pairs of numbers especially coordinates.

Quiz: What does relationship mean?

  1. To act in a manner such that one has fun; to engage in activities expressly for the purpose of recreation or entertainment.; To perform in (a sport); to participate in (a game).
  2. Something that is infinite in nature.; Indefinably large, countlessly great; immense.
  3. To bend back (light, etc.) from a surface.; To be bent back (light, etc.) from a surface.
