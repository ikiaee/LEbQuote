Vocabulary Focus: succeed

Definition: accomplish a goal or aim.

Quiz: What does succeed mean?

  1. accomplish a goal or aim.
  2. A challenge to prove courage.; The quality of daring; venturesomeness; boldness.
  3. definite but not specified or identified.
  4. The effect upon the judgment or feelings produced by any event, whether witnessed or participated in; personal and direct impressions as contrasted with description or fancies; personal acquaintance; actual enjoyment or suffering.; An activity one has performed.
Answer: A) accomplish a goal or aim.
