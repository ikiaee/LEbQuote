Vocabulary Focus: willing

Definition: the act of making a choice.

Quiz: What does willing mean?

  1. Thought; gerund of think.
  2. The state of being kind.; An instance of kind or charitable behaviour.
  3. the act of making a choice.
  4. Construction using whatever was available at the time.; Something constructed using whatever was available at the time.
Answer: C) the act of making a choice.

Vocabulary Focus: awkward

Definition: causing inconvenience.

Quiz: What does awkward mean?

  1. causing inconvenience.
  2. The image-making power of the mind; the act of mentally creating or reproducing an object not previously perceived; the ability to create such images.; Particularly, construction of false images; fantasizing.
  3. The state or characteristic of being just or fair.; The ideal of fairness, impartiality, etc., especially with regard to the punishment of wrongdoing.
  4. state of well-being characterized by emotions ranging from contentment to intense joy.
Answer: A) causing inconvenience.
