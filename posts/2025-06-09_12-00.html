Quiz: What does Knowledge mean? A) To make real; to convert from the imaginary or fictitious into reality; to bring into real existence; To become aware of (a fact or situation, especially of something that has been true for a long time). B) A construction or natural feature that spans a divide.; An arch or superstructure. C) the psychological result of perception and learning and reasoning. D) anything indispensable. Answer: C) the psychological result of perception and learning and reasoning.
