Vocabulary Focus: garden

Definition: An outdoor area containing one or more types of plants, usually plants grown for food or ornamental purposes.; The grounds at the front or back of a house.

Quiz: What does garden mean?

  1. Something that happens to someone by chance, a chance occurrence, especially a favourable one.; A superstitious feeling that brings fortune or success.
  2. A member of the British Army (as opposed to a member of the Territorial Army or Reserve).; A frequent, routine visitor to an establishment.
  3. the place where something begins, where it springs into being.
  4. An outdoor area containing one or more types of plants, usually plants grown for food or ornamental purposes.; The grounds at the front or back of a house.
Answer: D) An outdoor area containing one or more types of plants, usually plants grown for food or ornamental purposes.; The grounds at the front or back of a house.
